Complete opt-in workflow image

The workflow below is the branded step-by-step process submitted with toll-free registration.

Opt-in type: Text / Web form checkbox consent. Opt-in occurs during authenticated patient registration.

Consent language captured at opt-in

Patients must check this consent box before account creation can be completed.

"By checking this box, I consent to receive SMS text messages from Oz Health for appointment reminders, lab results, and care alerts. Message frequency: up to 5 msgs/week. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help."
Required action

Submit is blocked until the checkbox is selected.

Legal links in context

Terms and Privacy are shown directly next to the consent checkbox.

Confirmation behavior

A confirmation SMS is sent immediately after opt-in.

Exact registration description used for submission: Patients register at app.ozehr.com and enter their mobile phone number. A required checkbox states: "By checking this box, I consent to receive SMS text messages from Oz Health for appointment reminders, lab results, and care alerts. Message frequency: up to 5 msgs/week. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help." Patients must check the box to complete registration. A confirmation SMS is sent immediately after opt-in. Terms: https://ozehr.com/terms. Privacy: https://ozehr.com/privacy.
