Electronic Health Records & Charting

A comprehensive patient chart with 35+ sections — from problem lists and medications to lab results and imaging. Everything your clinicians need, organized and accessible.

  • SOAP notes, problem lists, medication lists, and allergy tracking
  • Family history, social history, and review of systems
  • Real-time collaborative editing so multiple staff can chart simultaneously
  • Full patient timeline with encounters, orders, and results
  • One-click access to any section from the chart sidebar

Appointment Scheduling

Powerful scheduling that handles walk-ins, recurring visits, and multi-provider calendars with ease. Students can self-schedule online 24/7.

  • Day, week, and month calendar views with drag-and-drop rescheduling
  • Online self-scheduling with real-time provider availability
  • Waitlist management with automated notification when slots open
  • Automated SMS and email appointment reminders
  • Schedule templates, room management, and holiday calendars

Clinical Documentation & AI Scribe

Spend less time typing and more time with patients. Our AI-powered scribe listens, transcribes, and formats clinical notes automatically.

  • Ambient voice-to-text transcription during patient encounters
  • Automatic SOAP note formatting from conversation
  • Smart phrases and text macros for common documentation patterns
  • Visual template builder for custom note types
  • E-signatures, addenda, and version history with full audit trail

Patient Portal & Online Booking

Give students 24/7 access to their health information. Book appointments, message providers, view lab results, and pay bills — all from one portal.

  • Self-service appointment booking with provider and time slot selection
  • Secure messaging with providers and clinical staff
  • Lab results viewing with reference ranges and trend charts
  • Bill pay, payment history, and insurance information
  • Pre-visit questionnaires and digital intake forms

Secure Messaging

HIPAA-compliant messaging between patients and providers, with internal staff messaging for care coordination.

  • Threaded conversations with patients and internal staff
  • File attachments for sharing documents and images
  • Message routing and priority levels for urgent communications
  • Quick-reply templates for common responses
  • Full audit trail for compliance and documentation

Billing & Revenue Cycle

From eligibility verification to payment posting, manage your entire revenue cycle without leaving the platform.

  • Real-time insurance eligibility verification (EDI 270/271)
  • Automated claim scrubbing and EDI 837 submission
  • ERA processing (835) with automatic payment posting
  • Denial management with appeal tracking and resubmission workflows
  • Patient statements, fee schedules, and aging reports

Laboratory & Imaging Orders

Order labs and imaging studies, track results, and flag critical values — all integrated directly into the patient chart.

  • LOINC-coded test ordering with searchable lab catalog
  • Lab partner directory with electronic order routing
  • Results inbox with abnormal value flagging and critical alerts
  • DICOM imaging integration for radiology studies
  • Historical result trends with comparison charts

Medications & E-Prescribing

Prescribe electronically — including controlled substances — with built-in drug interaction checking and pharmacy routing.

  • Comprehensive drug database with SIG builder
  • Real-time drug-drug and drug-allergy interaction alerts
  • EPCS for Schedule II-V controlled substances with 2FA
  • Pharmacy directory with campus pharmacy routing
  • Medication reconciliation and refill management

Immunization Compliance Tracking

Purpose-built for college enrollment compliance. Automate immunization verification, manage holds, and track campus-wide readiness.

  • Automated verification against state-specific requirements
  • Student clearance and hold management with SIS integration
  • Bulk compliance dashboards for enrollment deadlines
  • Digital waiver and medical exemption processing
  • FERPA-compliant records with complete audit trail

Analytics & Reporting

From revenue dashboards to population health insights, get the data you need to run your health center effectively.

  • Provider productivity metrics and utilization reports
  • Quality measures reporting (MIPS/MACRA compliance)
  • Population health insights with care gap identification
  • Custom report builder with CSV and PDF export

  • Integrated into the appointment scheduling workflow

  • Automatic visit documentation with AI-assisted notes
