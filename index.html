Questions, answered.

Short, honest answers for campus leaders, clinicians, and IT.

Is OzEHR built specifically for college health?

Yes. OzEHR is designed around campus workflows like walk-ins, immunization requirements, mental health coordination, and multi-location scheduling.

Can we migrate from our current system?

We support structured migrations and can help you map data, clean it, and validate it before go-live. The exact scope depends on your current system and exports.

What does campus IT usually care about?

SSO, audit logging, permissions, data access, and integrations. OzEHR is designed to make those conversations straightforward.

Do you offer demos and sandbox environments?

Yes. We can walk through campus scenarios and provide a tailored demo for your clinic team.
