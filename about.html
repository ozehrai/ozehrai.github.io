Modernizing College Health Technology

We believe every student deserves access to healthcare through software that's as modern as the rest of their lives.

Our Mission

College health centers serve millions of students each year with software that hasn't fundamentally changed in decades. We started Oz Health to fix that. Our platform brings the same quality of technology that students experience from consumer apps to the clinical environment — without compromising on security, compliance, or clinical depth.

Our Values

Innovation

We build with the latest technology, not on top of legacy systems. AI, real-time collaboration, and mobile-native architecture are foundations, not afterthoughts.

Security

Healthcare data demands the highest protection. HIPAA, FERPA, and SOC 2 compliance is built into every layer of our architecture.

Accessibility

Software should work for everyone — clinicians, students, and administrators. We design for clarity, ease of use, and WCAG compliance.

Student-First

Every design decision passes one test: does this make the student's healthcare experience better?
