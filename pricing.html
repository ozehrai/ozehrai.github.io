Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a setup fee?

No. All plans include free onboarding, data migration assistance, and training for your staff. We want to make switching as painless as possible.

Can you migrate data from Medicat, Titanium, or PointnClick?

Yes. We have dedicated migration specialists who handle data import from all major college health EHR systems. We support C-CDA, HL7, and custom CSV formats.

Is Oz Health FERPA compliant?

Absolutely. Oz Health is both HIPAA and FERPA compliant, with separate access controls for medical and counseling records, complete audit logging, and configurable data retention policies.

Do you offer academic pricing?

Yes. We offer special pricing for educational institutions. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your student population and provider count.

How long does implementation take?

Most health centers are fully live within 6-8 weeks. This includes data migration, staff training, workflow configuration, and a parallel-run period to ensure everything works perfectly.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a guided demo environment where you can explore the full platform with sample data. Schedule a demo and we'll set up your sandbox within 24 hours.

What happens if we need to scale mid-contract?

You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll prorate the difference and apply it to your current billing cycle. No penalties, no waiting.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include email support with a response time under 4 hours. Professional plans get priority support, and Enterprise plans include 24/7 phone support with a dedicated customer success manager.
