For Student Health Centers

Student health centers see a unique mix of cases — from seasonal flu to mental health screenings to sports physicals. Oz Health adapts to your clinical workflow with quick-charting templates, integrated lab ordering, and e-prescribing that routes to your campus pharmacy.

  • Walk-in and appointment scheduling with real-time availability
  • Quick-charting templates for common student visits (cold/flu, injuries, STI testing, wellness exams)
  • Integrated lab ordering with campus and commercial laboratories
  • E-prescribing with campus pharmacy routing
  • Automated after-visit summaries sent to the student portal
  • Same-day telehealth for after-hours concerns

For Counseling Centers

Campus counseling centers need documentation tools that match their clinical model — progress notes for therapy, standardized assessments, and strict access controls that keep counseling records separate from medical records.

  • Progress note templates for individual therapy, group therapy, and psychiatric visits
  • Standardized assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, AUDIT, Columbia Suicide Severity)
  • Crisis documentation and safety planning tools
  • Separate record access controls — counseling records stay isolated from medical
  • Group therapy session management and attendance tracking
  • Referral tracking to external providers with follow-up alerts

Typical Workflow

Student requests appointment via portal → intake forms completed digitally → counselor reviews PHQ-9 scores before session → session notes captured with AI assist → follow-up scheduled automatically.

For Health Compliance Offices

Every fall, compliance offices process thousands of immunization records under tight enrollment deadlines. Oz Health automates verification, manages holds, and gives you real-time dashboards so you always know where you stand.

  • Automated immunization verification against state-specific requirements
  • Student clearance and hold management with SIS integration
  • Bulk compliance status dashboards for enrollment deadlines
  • Digital waiver and medical/religious exemption processing
  • Compliance reporting for accreditation reviews
  • FERPA-compliant data handling with complete audit trails

Typical Workflow

Incoming students upload immunization records → system auto-verifies against requirements → holds placed for non-compliant students → students notified via portal and SMS → compliance dashboard shows real-time enrollment readiness.
