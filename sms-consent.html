SMS Communication Consent

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Oz Health provides healthcare-related SMS notifications as part of our patient portal and EHR platform. This page explains what messages you may receive, how to opt in and opt out, and your rights regarding SMS communications from Oz Health.

1. What Messages Will You Receive?

By opting in to Oz Health SMS notifications, you may receive the following types of transactional, healthcare-related messages:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Lab result availability notifications
  • Care-related messages from your provider
  • Account and portal notifications
  • Prescription refill reminders

All messages are transactional in nature and relate directly to your healthcare. No marketing or promotional messages are sent via SMS.

2. How to Opt In

You can opt in to receive SMS notifications from Oz Health in any of the following ways:

  • During patient portal registration: Check the "I consent to receive SMS notifications from Oz Health" checkbox when creating your account.
  • In your portal settings: Provide your mobile phone number and enable SMS preferences under Settings > Communications.
  • By texting START: Send the keyword START to our messaging number to opt in.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your appointment schedule and care activity.

  • Typical frequency: 1 to 5 messages per week
  • Daily cap: Maximum of 5 messages per day
  • Quiet hours: No messages are sent between 9:00 PM and 8:00 AM in your local timezone

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

4. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Your carrier's standard messaging rates apply. Oz Health does not charge for SMS notifications.

Contact your wireless carrier if you have questions about your messaging plan or data charges.

5. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of Oz Health SMS notifications at any time using any of the following methods:

  • Reply STOP to any message you receive from Oz Health
  • Update your preferences in the Oz Health patient portal under Settings > Communications
  • Contact your provider's office and request to be removed from SMS notifications

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message from Oz Health acknowledging your request. No further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

7. Your Privacy

Protecting your health information is our top priority. Oz Health SMS communications are designed with the following safeguards:

  • HIPAA compliant: Messages never contain sensitive health details visible on lock screens or notification previews
  • No third-party sharing: We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing or any other purpose
  • Minimum necessary standard: All message content follows the HIPAA minimum necessary standard, containing only the information needed for the specific notification

Read our full Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Read our Terms & Conditions for the complete terms governing your use of our services.

8. Healthcare Compliance

  • Oz Health is HIPAA, FERPA, and SOC 2 compliant
  • SMS communications are limited to transactional healthcare messages only
  • No marketing messages are sent via SMS
  • All message content follows the HIPAA minimum necessary standard
  • Message logs are maintained in accordance with healthcare record retention requirements
